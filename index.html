# man man¶

(This document was last updated on 2019-05-08 at 14:08)

If you are new to Ubuntu system administration, this is the place to start. We document in this ‘recipe book’ the common tasks to manage Ubuntu machines based on our experience on using them in our research group. Ubuntu is our linux distribution of choice because it is widely supported by the community and is used in physical and virtualized environments.

Supported Ubuntu Version: 16.04 LTS (64-bit)
